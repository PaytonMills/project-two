Listen to Our Radio

In 1994, the staff of UNC’s SunSITE, the first Web site in North America, helped WXYC become the first radio station in the world to stream its on-air signal live over the Internet. Since then, listenership has greatly increased. WXYC is now broadcasted with a radius of 15 miles from the station.

FAQ

What station can I find WXYC on?

  • If you’re in the Chapel Hill area, tune your FM receiver to 89.3 MHz.
  • For remote listeners, select one of the MP3 or OGG streams.

Where can I hear WXYC?

  • The WXYC broadcast tower is located on Jones Ferry Road west of Carrboro; coverage extends into the near sides of Durham, Wake and Chatham Counties.

Who decides what plays on WXYC?

  • We don’t allow a computer to program music for us; each set is put together by a DJ who selects music from our library of tens of thousands of CDs and LPs. Check out our current DJ schedule.
  • WXYC is a free-format radio station, which means that in an hour you’ll hear a decisively non-block mix of any and all types of 20th and 21st century music. We don’t separate our programs by genre — almost all shows on WXYC are a wide-ranging mix of styles and eras, although we tend to favor music that doesn’t get much airplay elsewhere in the Triangle area.

What makes WXYC special?

  • On weeknights and Sundays we feature specialty programs from artist features to talk shows.
  • All of our music comes from physical copies. We have an extensive record, cassette, and CD collection that spans several decades and hundreds of genres

Can I request songs?

  • Yes! Call in and request a song for a chance to hear it.

How do I get my music on WXYC?

  • We have a new release playbox from which DJs must play five to six cuts per hour. The contents of the playbox are updated weekly with releases chosen by our music director and the music department staff. We try to create a playbox that is representative of diverse genres, including local artists, singles and reissues. To send new music to us, please address your envelope to the following address:
    • WXYC Music Department CB# 5210, Carolina Union Chapel Hill, NC 2759
